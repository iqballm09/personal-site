About Me

Muhammad Iqbal was born in Bukittinggi on September 11, 1999. He is a learner who has a passion for learning new stuffs related to IT, ranging from Data Analytics, Machine Learning, Software Enginneering, to Blockchain Techology. Currently, he is focusing on Software Engineering and Blockchain field.

He studied Computer Science at Institut Pertanian Bogor (IPB) starting on August 2019 and graduated with cum laude honors on November 2023. His thesis on the field of machine learning was titled "Image-Based Modeling of Rubber Plant Disease Severity Multispectral Drone Image Using Random Forest Algorithm". Before studying at IPB, he took a gap year for 1 year and relocated to Kampung Inggris Pare, Kediri, East Java for learning english and preparing entrance exam.

Besides coding, he has hobbies such as playing game, reading manga and novels.
